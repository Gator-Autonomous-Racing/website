Our Mission

Gator Autonomous Racing seeks to design, build, and race high-performance autonomous vehicles, providing University of Florida students with innovative, hands-on experience in robotics, artificial intelligence, controls, and embedded systems engineering. Through national and international competitions, research-driven development, and community outreach, Gator Autonomous Racing aims to advance the field of autonomous mobility while developing future engineers and leaders in engineering and technology.
